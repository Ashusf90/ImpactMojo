About ImpactMojo

Free development education for impact practitioners across South Asia

Our Mission

ImpactMojo is a free educational platform for development professionals, NGOs, and impact practitioners across South Asia. We provide practical, accessible courses on MEAL, research methods, impact evaluation, and more – designed by practitioners, for practitioners.

Read about our plans for the future in our blog post: What's Coming in 2026

Beyond Courses

We also offer interactive labs for hands-on practice, educational games that make complex concepts engaging, premium tools for advanced practitioners, one-on-one coaching, and organizational workshops tailored to your team's needs.

"Pay what you think is fair. Learn what you need to know. Create the change you want to see."

All core educational content on ImpactMojo is and will remain permanently free. We believe that financial barriers should never prevent someone from accessing quality development education.

Our Sustainability Model

💚 Accessibility First

Education should be free at the point of access for those who need it most.

🛠️ Practice Over Theory

Real-world applicability and practical skills take priority over abstract concepts.

🤝 Community Driven

Built by practitioners, for practitioners, with continuous feedback integration.

🌏 South Asia Context

Content designed specifically for the realities of development work in our region.
