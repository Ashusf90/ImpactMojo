About ImpactMojo

Free development education for impact practitioners across South Asia

Our Mission

ImpactMojo is a free educational platform designed for development professionals, NGOs, social enterprises, government agencies, foundations, and individual practitioners across South Asia.

We believe that high-quality development education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their organization's budget or their personal financial situation.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic theory and field practice, providing practical, actionable knowledge that development practitioners can apply immediately in their work. From monitoring and evaluation frameworks to gender analysis, from research methods to impact assessment, we cover the full spectrum of skills needed for effective development practice.

Beyond Courses

We also offer interactive labs for hands-on practice, educational games that make complex concepts engaging, premium tools for advanced practitioners, one-on-one coaching, and organizational workshops tailored to your team's needs.

Our Philosophy

"Pay what you think is fair. Learn what you need to know. Create the change you want to see."

All core educational content on ImpactMojo is and will remain permanently free. We believe that financial barriers should never prevent someone from accessing quality development education.

Our sustainability model relies on:

  • Pay-what-you-can donations from learners who can afford to contribute
  • Premium tools and services for advanced practitioners
  • Organizational workshops and training programs
  • Sponsorships and partnerships with like-minded organizations

Accessibility First

Education should be free at the point of access for those who need it most.

Practice Over Theory

Real-world applicability and practical skills take priority over abstract concepts.

Community Driven

Built by practitioners, for practitioners, with continuous feedback integration.

South Asia Context

Content designed specifically for the realities of development work in our region.
